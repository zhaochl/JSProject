Informer - Skateboarding Basics

Skateboarding has increased considerably in the past few years as a major sport among America's youth. Although the equipment involved in skateboarding is fairly straightforward, there is still some degree of education involved for newcomers to the sport. Regardless of what type of skateboarding you plan on doing, you will no doubt need theh core components of a skateboard, which consist of a deck, trucks, and wheels. Additionally, it's a good idea to consider a helmet and pads, especially if you plan on skating any tall obstacles such as vert ramps and concrete bowls.
